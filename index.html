Key Features

What does Hearth have to offer?

Prevents Cold Starts

Allows users to pick how often they want their AWS Lambda functions to be warmed.

Metrics Visualization

Visualize real-time data such as duration, memory, and billing in graphs.

AWS Lambda Logs

Easy parsing of most recent logs to detect cold starts.

  • Warming Functions

  • View Logs & Graphs in Dashboard

    Monitor invocations in detail through Hearth.
